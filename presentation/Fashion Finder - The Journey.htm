The Search Quest

The Broken System

Searching for clothing online is heavily reliant on manual keywords and rigid tagging systems.

  • Keyword Failure: "Blue casual top" returns thousands of irrelevant results.
  • Lost Context: Users know what they want visually, but can't type it.
  • Bad UX: Rigid filters fail to capture style or silhouette.

The Semantic Solution

Replacing text guesswork with absolute visual precision and AI feature extraction.

  • See It, Find It: Upload a photo to retrieve visual matches instantly.
  • Deep Understanding: The AI analyzes garment color, texture, and fit dynamically.
  • Beyond Tags: Recognizes abstract concepts like style and formality automatically.
